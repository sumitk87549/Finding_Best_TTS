Dimaag hi toh asli power hai, jo sab kuch banaata aur shape karta hai.
Aur insaan bhi dimaag se bana hai, aur hamesha use karta rehta hai.
Soch ka tool letaa hai, aur apni marzi ke hisaab se,
hazaar khushiyaan paida kar letaa hai, hazaar dukkhon mein gir jaata hai.

Woh andar hi sochtaa hai, aur phir woh sach ho jaata hai.
Hamaare aas paas jo bhi hota hai, bas humaari hi reflection hoti hai.

Yeh choti si book, thought ki power ke baare mein ek complete guide nahi hai.
Is pe bahut likha jaa chuka hai.
Yeh sirf suggest karti hai, explain nahi karti.
Iska aim yeh hai ki logon ko motivate karein.
Aur unko yeh pata chale ki sach toh yeh hai,
hum khud hi apne banaate hain.

Apni soch se, jo hum choose karte aur encourage karte hain, uske hisaab se.
Dimaag hi toh sab kuch bunta hai.
Andar ke character ka kapda bhi, aur baahar ki circumstances ka kapda bhi.
Aur agar ab tak tune andhere mein buna ho, toh ab tu roshni mein, khushi se bun sakti hai.
