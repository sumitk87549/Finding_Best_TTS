Dimag hi toh asli power hai, jo sab kuch banata aur shape karta hai,
Aur insaan bhi dimag se bana hai, aur hamesha use karta rehta hai.
Soch ka tool leta hai, aur apni marzi ke hisaab se…
Hazaar khushiyan paida kar leta hai, hazar dukho mein gir jaata hai 
Woh andar hi sochta hai, aur phir woh sach ho jata hai:
Around us jo bhi hota hai, bas humari reflection hoti hai.
Yeh choti si book (yeh meditation aur life experience se mili hui baatein hain) thought ki power ke bare mein ek complete guide nahi hai.  Is pe bahut likha jaa chuka hai…
Yeh sirf suggest karti hai, explain nahi karti. Iska aim yeh hai ki logon ko motivate karein…
aur unko yeh pata chale ki sach toh yeh hai ki –
“Hum khud hi apne banate hain.”
Apni soch se jo hum choose karte aur encourage karte hain uske hisaab se. Dimag hi toh sab kuch bunta hai,
Andar ke character ka garment bhi, aur bahar ki circumstances ka garment bhi.
Aur agar ab tak tune andhere mein buni ho… to ab tu roshni mein khushi se bun sakti hai.
